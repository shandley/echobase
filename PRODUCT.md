# EchoBase — Product Context

## Product Purpose
A unified genomics knowledge platform for Chiroptera (bat) biology. Integrates genome assemblies, protein sequences, and scientific literature. Named for bat echolocation: send a query, get biological context back.

## Users
Bat biologists, comparative genomicists, evolutionary biologists. PhD researchers and graduate students. Technically proficient; impatient with bad UX; will leave immediately if the data feels untrustworthy or hard to read.

## Register
product

## Tone
Precise. Confident. Restrained. This is a research instrument, not a product landing page. No marketing language. Data earns trust; copy stays out of the way.

## Anti-references
- Generic bioinformatics portals (NCBI, Ensembl): too dated, too cluttered
- Dark SaaS dashboards with neon accents: too startup
- Gaming UIs: any glow, bloom, or aggressive gradients
- Science communication sites: too light, too accessible, wrong audience

## Strategic principles
- Data density over whitespace aesthetics
- Every UI element earns its place
- Researchers should feel like they found a serious tool, not a beautiful app
